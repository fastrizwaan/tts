HOW TO
FOLD A T-SHIRT

1 Lay the T-shirt face down on a table. Smooth it flat.

2 Fold over one side and sleeve from top to bottom, taking a line between the collar and the shoulder seam.

3 Repeat the same action on the other side.

4 At this point, women’s T-shirts can be folded in half. Men’s T-shirts tend to be longer and benefit taking an extra fold before folding again into a square and turning over.
