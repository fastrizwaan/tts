HOW TO
BE A GOOD FRIEND

• Be a good listener.

• Know when to offer advice and when to keep quiet.

• Be empathetic.

• Support people when they’re ill, or feeling down.

• Be generous with your time; if life’s too busy, ring or text.

• Remember to ask people about things that are important to them.

• Forgive their shortcomings; you have them, too. We are all only human.

• Celebrate people’s successes.

• Keep their secrets.

• Remember their birthday.

• Refrain from embarrassing or upstaging others.

• Pay back money on time; don’t borrow significant sums of money.

• Don’t be jealous; no bitching about people behind their back.

• Clear the air if you fall out with someone; don’t hold a grudge.

• Commandeering people’s other friends and seeing them behind their back is a no-no.

• Don’t do, or say, things that might hurt others.

• Don’t steal boyfriends/girlfriends!
