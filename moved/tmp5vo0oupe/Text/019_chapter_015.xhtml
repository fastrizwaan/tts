HOW TO
GUT A FISH

1 Place the fish on its side on a chopping board (preferably one color-coded blue for fish), or on its back if it is a big fish. Wear latex gloves if youâ€™d rather. Take a sharp knife and slice the fish open as shown.

2 Insert the knife through the gills and slit through the skin flap.
